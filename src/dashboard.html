What is a DRG?

Medicare severity diagnosis-related group (MS-DRG) is a classification system used by Medicare to categorize inpatient hospital stays based on diagnosis, severity, and resource utilization.

Average Total Payment

The average total payments to all providers for the MS-DRG

Average Covered Charges

The provider's average charge for services covered by Medicare for all discharges in the MS-DRG.

Average Medicare Payments

The average amount that Medicare pays to the provider for Medicare's share of the MS-DRG.
